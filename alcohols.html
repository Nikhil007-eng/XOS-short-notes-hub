üí° Core Concepts & JEE Tips

JEE Focus: Memorize reagent selectivity, product identification, and reaction conditions for quick problem-solving.
  • Grignard Reagent (RMgX): Source of carbanion. Always requires aqueous workup after reaction.
  • Oxidation States: 1¬∞ alcohol ‚Üí aldehyde ‚Üí acid; 2¬∞ ‚Üí ketone; 3¬∞ ‚Üí no easy oxidation.
  • Popoff's Rule: In unsymmetrical ketone oxidation, carbonyl stays with smaller alkyl group.
  • Carbene (:CX2): Electrophile from CHCl3 + OH-.
  • Organometallics: More ionic character = more reactive (RMgX > R2CuLi).
